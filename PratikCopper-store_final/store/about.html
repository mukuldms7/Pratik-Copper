History

How it’s started?

In 1945 Shri Kakubhai Barot (Grandfather – Pankaj/Sudhir) was came in Pune and started job in Premaji Lilachand (Big Traders in copper utensils). 7 years later Shri Raghunatbhai Barot join with Kakubhai in same firm. After 4/5 years the owner of Premaji Lilachand made them working partners. ¾ years later partnership was dissolved and Raghunatbhai decided to moved out and start their own business.

Who started?

Raghunatbhai started this own business as Kakubhai & Sons in 1960 in their own purchased shop 234 Raviwar Peth Pune. Started as commission agent for copper utensils. 1965 Kakubhai passed away and Raghunatbhai continued this business till 1990. In 1992 Raghunatbhai started their own first manufacturing unit with two products.

Initial products?

Tamhan and Gadva in 1992 with manual karagir work.

Time line

1992 to 2001 : Shri Sudhir Barot is on board and started their first manufacturing unit at Sukhsagar Nager Pune with 2 machines. Established new firm Shubham Metal.

2004/05 started additional products range with additional six machines at Sukhsagaer.

2012 : After completion of BE-Mechanical Mr. Pratik Barot on board and start handling Sukhsagar Nagar’s production department.

2015 : Mr. Shridhar Barot and Mr. Ravi Barot joined and started handling Sales and Purchase departments from their Raviwar Peth shop.

Product development

1992 to 2000 : Tamhan, Gadwa.

2001 to 2008 : Apple Handa, Kulfi Matka, Ma Matla, Navranga Handa/Kalshi, Pawali, Ghanhal etc.

2012/13 : Kagu Handa, Flower pot with total 22 machines and more than 40 workers.

2014/15 : All types of copper utensils with 100+ products.
